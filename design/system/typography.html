Helvetica / Arial

We choose Helvetica as a main font because of its friendly, professional, and classical typeface. It is also used in graphic design and web design all over the place. Arial is a very similar font to Helvetica. To an untrained eye, the differences between the two fonts is almost undetectable.

Its belly was white, and its legs shaded from the slate of its shoulders and hips to a vivid yellow at the feet.
